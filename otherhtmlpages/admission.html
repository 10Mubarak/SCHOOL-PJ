Admissions

At Golden Age Academy, we believe every child deserves a learning environment where they can grow academically, socially, and creatively. Our admissions process is designed to be simple, transparent, and supportive for families. Whether you are enrolling for the first time or transferring from another school, our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Admission Overview

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds who demonstrate curiosity, respect, and a willingness to learn. Golden Age Academy accepts learners into Pre-Nursery, Nursery, Primary, and Junior Secondary classes. Admission decisions are based on age suitability, available space, academic readiness, and an informal interview or assessment.

Entry Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or age verification
  • Recent passport photographs
  • Previous school report (where applicable)
  • Medical/Immunization records

Fees & Scholarships

Golden Age Academy offers competitive tuition fees that reflect the high-quality education and facilities provided. Limited scholarships and financial support are available for students who demonstrate exceptional academic potential or financial need.

How to Apply

  1. Download and fill out the application form.
  2. Submit required documents and supporting records.
  3. Schedule an assessment or interview for placement.
  4. Await admission confirmation from the school.
